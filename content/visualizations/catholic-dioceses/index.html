This is a map of Roman Catholic dioceses in North America, from the establishment of the first diocese in 1511 to the present.

Year: 1800

How to use this visualization: Adjust the slider to control the year displayed in the map. Hover over points on the map to see details about each diocese or archdiocese. The bar chart also displays the number of dioceses established in your selected decade.
